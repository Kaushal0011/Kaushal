Hi I'm Savaliya Kaushalkumar a curious mind passionate about Blockchain, Cybersecurity, and Cryptography.

Over time, I’ve built strong skills in areas such as zero-knowledge proofs, consensus algorithms, and secure system design. I enjoy diving deep into research problems and exploring how cryptographic principles can be applied to real-world tech.

I enjoy blending development, cybersecurity, and research to create secure, future-ready solutions in the fast-evolving world of decentralized technology.
